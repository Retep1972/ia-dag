You just scanned an untrusted QR code.

In cybersecurity and OT environments, curiosity is an attack surface.

QR phishing (quishing) is increasingly used for:

  • Credential theft
  • MFA harvesting
  • Malware delivery
  • Fake login portals

Even cybersecurity professionals scan things impulsively — especially when something promises “extra resources”.
